A Little About Me

Hi! My name is Benny Zuckier, but Benny Zuke sounds better. I'm a creative and a tinkerer which led me to become a photographer and make (design and code!) this website. My love for photography started when I was eight when my late grandfather bought me a camera for my birthday - I can't stop taking pictures and really enjoy editing them. I worked for a while as a magician and love "the hussle" - hire me for your next event or photo need and you'll love the results.
